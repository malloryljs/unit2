Wisconsin Central Sands Well Depth-to-water in 2019

The data used in the map represents the distance from the surface of a well to the level of water present in the well in 2019.
This can be an indicator of overall water usage in a specfic aquifer system, and whether a cone of depression may be present in the aquifer.
The larger the circle, the lower the water level in the well. It's important to note that wells vary in total depth and elevation, so some sites may not be correctly represented.
Months are represented by their number (5 = May, 6 = June,...)
